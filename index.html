About

I'm a Product Design Lead with over 20 years of experience in design, I’m passionate about creating impactful digital experiences. My journey started in 2003 after completing a BSc in Computing and Management, followed by an MSc in Business and Enterprise, and an MA in Digital Effects. Along the way, I've had the privilege of contributing to award-winning projects, including a BAFTA for British Short Animation. Currently, I take on Lead, Principle and Senior Product Designer roles, blending my love for hands-on design with mentoring and guiding other designers. I’m driven by the power of pixels and the satisfaction of helping teams achieve their creative potential.
