About

Hi, I'm Ollie. I am dedicated to creating experiences that delight and connect with users. Enjoying my work and creating something that is worthwhile is incredibly important to me. I have spent over fifteen years in industry designing interfaces for very high profile products and web projects, including as lead web designer on a publishing platform that served over 75 million web pageviews per month.

I also have experience in and around the visual effects industry where I gained a key understanding of VFX and animation workflows and artists’ needs, both as my time as a lighting artist in a studio and as a product designer with many collaborative discussions with clients about the issues and problems that challenge them on a daily basis.

These experiences have taught me how to work collaboratively, problem solve and accept critique from professionals in large project teams. Throughout my career I have developed many style guides and design libraries to aid consistency and promote best practices and ultimately deliver on those improvements.
